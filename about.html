ABOUT US

International Commodity Trading Company

Baltic Alliance

Baltic Alliance is a major marketer of commodity petrochemicals and competes with other firms in the sale or purchase of crude oil and petroleum products and services in many national and international markets and employs all methods of competition which are lawful and appropriate for such purposes. Our principal business is energy, involving exploration for, and production of, crude oil and natural gas, manufacture of petroleum products and transportation and sale of crude oil, natural gas and petroleum products.

We understand the energy and petrochemical industries are highly competitive, hence the need for competition with other industries in supplying the energy, fuel and chemical needs of both industrial and individual consumers. In the past year, the company exported 57 million barrels of crude oil with its JV partners, and traded just above 3 million metric tonnes of refined petroleum products.

As an independent entity, Baltic Alliance is fully empowered to chart its own course, make independent decisions, and forge strategic partnerships that align with the vision and objectives to leverage our expertise, experience, and resources to better meet the needs of our valued customers, stakeholders, and the community at large.
